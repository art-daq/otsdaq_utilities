Instructions

To read or write:
1. Select one or more FECs from the list of FECs;
2. Enter the target address above. If you choose to write, you also need to enter the data;
3. Select the format of your address and data (hex/dec). If you choose to read, the data format selected will be expected.
4. Select either "Write" or "Read" to finish your manual command.
